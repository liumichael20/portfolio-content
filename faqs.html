Frequently Asked Questions

Where Do We Take The Shoot?

My standard location is at Ross, where we'll take shots in various locations (minimum 3) both inside and outside the building.

If you have a request for a different location, contact me!

What Should I Wear?

Most opt to wear a classic suit, tie or no tie is fine. If you don't have a suit or are going for a more casual look, I'd suggest clothing with minimal patterns, as that texture can distract from your headshot.

How Many Photos Will I Get?

I typically take 200-400 photos for a single client every session. I'll go over them and pick around 30 of the best photos to send to you. Among those 30, I will edit and retouch 5-10 photos.

What If I'm Not Fully Satisfied?

If you're not fully satisfied, don't worry. I offer an 100% refund policy. Simply let me know and you'll get your money back, no questions asked.
